📌 Title

Evaluation of a Generative AI System for Localized Climate Awareness and Behavioral Change

📊 Status

✅ Approved

📖 Overview

Climate change communication often relies on generic, non-localized content that fails to address regional differences, cultural contexts, and language diversity. This project explores how Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) can be leveraged to create personalized, region-specific climate awareness content that is easier to understand and more effective in encouraging behavioral change.

The project focuses on designing and evaluating a Generative AI–based system that produces localized climate communication tailored to specific audiences.

❗ Problem Statement

Despite the availability of extensive climate data, current awareness strategies face several challenges:

Lack of localized and context-aware content
Overuse of technical and inaccessible language
Difficulty in motivating behavioral change
High cost and effort required for manual content customization
Limited scalability of traditional communication methods
🎯 Objectives
Understand the role of Generative AI in climate communication
Analyze limitations of existing generic awareness approaches
Design a system for localized and audience-specific content generation
Evaluate effectiveness in improving awareness and comprehension
Assess impact on climate-friendly behavioral change
💡 Proposed Solution

This project proposes a Generative AI–driven framework that:

Generates region-specific climate awareness content
Adapts messaging based on audience characteristics
Uses structured prompts and curated datasets
Produces human-like, easy-to-understand communication
